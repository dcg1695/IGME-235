About Me

One of my hobbies is playing video games (you know GDD majors teehee), but another one of my hobbies, that's also my favorite, is singing and music. Music, including pop, anything PTX, etc., has definitely helped me with feeling more confident and expressive with myself. During the time that I'm at RIT, I want to be able to learn what making games entails, from the mechanics to the story to characters and actual gameplay. Each aspect of building a game is so important, especially the programming, and I also want to really hone my skills and excel at programming. From this class, I want to know what goes into building sites and understand a lot about HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
